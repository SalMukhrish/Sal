Startup Roadmap: From Concept to Scale

Phase 1: Idea & Validation

Identify a real problem, understand the target audience, and validate assumptions through lean experiments (like MMVL) using research and customer interviews.

Phase 2: MVP Development

Translate concepts into a functional MVP, prioritizing core features and preparing for user testing to gather real-world feedback.

Phase 3: Early Traction

Acquire initial users, analyze behavior, and iterate to achieve product-market fit, focusing on engagement and retention metrics.

Phase 4: Scaling & GTM

Scale operations, build sales and marketing funnels, and execute a go-to-market strategy to expand the user base.

Phase 5: Growth & Optimization

Monitor KPIs, optimize processes, explore new revenue streams, and plan for expansion, focusing on sustainable growth.
